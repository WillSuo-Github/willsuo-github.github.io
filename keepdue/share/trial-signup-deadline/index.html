Shareable Reminder Habit

Before you close the trial signup screen, capture one date.

KeepDue is a manual-first iPhone reminder app for recording trial and renewal deadlines before they become surprise charges. No bank link. No account.

The habit this visual explains

  1. Record the renewal or cancellation date while you are still on the signup screen.
  2. Pick a decision day before the final charge.
  3. Use a local reminder instead of linking a bank account.
