Help for the current launch build.

KeepDue helps you catch subscription renewals and free-trial conversions before they charge, without linking your bank account.

Last updated: 2026-04-24

What the current version does

  • tracks subscriptions manually
  • shows upcoming renewals
  • shows monthly burn at a glance
  • sends local reminders for renewals and free trials
  • supports up to 5 active subscriptions for free on one device

Common questions

  • I am not receiving reminders.
    Make sure notification permission is enabled for KeepDue, the renewal or trial date is still in the future, and the reminder still appears as scheduled inside the app.
  • Why can I only track 5 subscriptions for free?
    KeepDue Free currently supports up to 5 active subscriptions on one device. The launch policy is to keep this limit truthful instead of promising a purchase path that does not exist yet. Read the free-limit guide.
  • Does the app link my bank account?
    No. The current version is manual-first and does not require bank linking.
  • Does the app import subscriptions automatically?
    No. The current version is designed as a simple manual-first renewal companion.
