What to record before closing the signup screen

  1. The service or app name.
  2. The exact renewal or cancellation date.
  3. The price after the trial ends.
  4. Whether the plan renews monthly or annually.
  5. A note about where to cancel if the signup screen shows it.

30-second setup

  1. Add the trial name.
  2. Add the renewal or cancellation date.
  3. Set the first reminder before the final day.
  4. Add a backup reminder for expensive trials or annual plans.
  5. Keep the entry somewhere that will notify you, not just store the note.

Where KeepDue fits

KeepDue is built for this manual reminder workflow. It lets you add a trial or subscription manually, see what is due next, and get local reminders before the charge. It does not require bank linking or an account.

KeepDue is free for up to 5 active subscriptions on one device. If you hit the current limit, read the free-limit guide.

Does this cancel the trial automatically?

No. KeepDue helps you remember the decision deadline; it does not cancel services for you.

Do I need to connect a bank account?

No. The current workflow is manual and local-first, so the reminder can be created without bank linking.

What reminder time should I use?

Use a reminder before the final day. For expensive trials, add a backup reminder.
