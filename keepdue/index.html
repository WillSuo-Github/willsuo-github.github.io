Manual-First Renewal Companion

Catch renewals before they charge.

KeepDue is a private iPhone app for people who want a simple way to track subscription renewals and free-trial conversions without linking a bank account.

What the current launch build does

  • Tracks subscriptions manually with fast template and custom-entry flows.
  • Shows what is due next and your projected monthly burn.
  • Sends local reminders for renewals and trial endings.
  • Supports up to 5 active subscriptions for free on one device.

What it does not pretend to do

  • No bank linking.
  • No account creation required for the core tracking flow.
  • No promise of sync, widgets, imports, or paid unlocks before they actually ship.
