Privacy Policy

Privacy without bank linking.

KeepDue is a manual-first subscription reminder app for Apple users. The current build keeps its core tracking flow local to your device and does not require account creation to do the basic job.

Last updated: 2026-04-24

Data you enter

In the current version, the subscriptions you add in the app are stored locally on your device.

  • subscription name
  • amount
  • billing cycle
  • renewal date
  • optional trial date
  • reminder lead time
  • visual tint choice

Notifications

If you allow notifications, KeepDue schedules local reminder notifications on your device.

Notification permission can be changed by you at any time in iOS Settings.

Analytics

The current app sends limited product-analytics events to Firebase Analytics to understand app usage and product behavior.

  • app opens and returns
  • whether reminders are enabled
  • subscription count and aggregated monthly burn
  • whether a subscription was added
  • archive, restore, and delete actions
  • reminder scheduling coverage and reminder interactions
  • free-tier limit exposure

Data sharing

The current version does not describe advertising, sale of personal data, or bank-data sharing because the shipped product does not rely on those surfaces.

If the shipped app changes materially, this policy must be updated before publication.
