# KeepDue Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-23

KeepDue is a manual-first subscription reminder app for Apple users.

## Data you enter

In the current version, the subscriptions you add in the app are stored locally on your device.

This can include:

- subscription name
- amount
- billing cycle
- renewal date
- optional trial date
- reminder lead time
- visual tint choice

The current app does not require account creation or bank linking to use its core tracking flow.

## Notifications

If you allow notifications, KeepDue schedules local reminder notifications on your device.

Notification permission can be changed by you at any time in iOS Settings.

## Analytics

The current app sends limited product-analytics events to Firebase Analytics.

These events are used to understand app usage and product behavior, such as:

- app opens and returns
- whether reminders are enabled
- subscription count and aggregated monthly burn
- whether a subscription was added
- archive, restore, and delete actions
- reminder scheduling coverage and reminder interactions
- free-tier limit exposure

The current app does not rely on bank-account access, statement import, or receipt scanning for this telemetry.

## Data sharing

The current version does not describe any advertising, sale of personal data, or bank-data sharing because the shipped product does not rely on those surfaces.

If the shipped app changes materially, this policy must be updated before publication.
