# KeepDue

Last updated: 2026-04-23

KeepDue is a manual-first subscription reminder app for Apple users who want to catch renewals and free-trial conversions before they charge.

## Pages

- [Privacy Policy](./privacy.md)
- [Support](./support.md)

## Product truth

- no bank linking
- no account creation required for the core tracking flow
- free for up to 5 active subscriptions on one device in the current launch posture
